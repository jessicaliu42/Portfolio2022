About Me

I'm a UI Designer with a B.S. in animation, a UX research and UI design certificate from Georgia Tech Professional Education, and five years experience of front-end developer working experience.

With a strong background in front-end engineering, I've collaborated with small teams to complete over 20 projects, including website pages and mobile apps, meeting deadlines and solving technical challenges.

My skills encompass HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and Figma. I specialize in web development for desktop, mobile, and tablet, as well as UI design, which includes user research, usability testing, information architecture, and interaction design.

In addition to my professional work, I have valuable experience as a Teaching Assistant (TA), where I've helped students grasp key concepts and develop their web development and UI design skills. Furthermore, I have served as a Code Teacher for kids, inspiring young learners to explore the world of coding and web development.

These experiences have enriched my ability to effectively communicate complex concepts, making me a valuable asset in project collaboration and educational roles.
